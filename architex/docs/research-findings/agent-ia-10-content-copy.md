- 30 CTA buttons, all starting with a verb, maximum 4 words, with style annotations (primary/secondary/ghost)
- 12 tooltips, each answering "what is this" in one sentence

- Practice activities: **Challenge** (not Problem, Exercise, Mission)

- Spaced repetition: **Review** (not Remember, Recall, Retain)
- Achievements: **Achievement** (not Badge, Award, Milestone)
- Daily activity: **Daily Review** (not Daily Drill, Daily Challenge)
