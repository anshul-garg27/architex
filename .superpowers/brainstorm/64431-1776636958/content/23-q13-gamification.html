Q13 · How loud is streak / XP / achievements in the new UI?

Your codebase has StreakCounter.tsx, 30+ achievements in 4 rarity tiers, and progress-store tracks XP. But interview-prep pros aren't 10-year-olds on Duolingo. Calibrate the vibe.

🎚️ GAMIFICATION VOLUME DIAL
🔊🔊🔊 Duolingo
XP burst, streak fire, league, gems, sound effects, push notifications
🔊🔊 Brilliant
Streak visible, progress bars, some celebration, no XP spray
🔊 LeetCode
Submission stats, streak count, badges, mostly passive
🔇 GitHub
Contribution graph. Achievements exist but feel incidental
A

Loud · Duolingo-style

XP spray on every correct answer. Level-up explosions. Daily streak push notifications. Sounds on checkpoint correct. Leaderboards.

Pros: Proven retention hook. Habit forming.
Cons: Feels juvenile to a 30-yo Staff Engineer prepping for Meta. Risks brand damage — "isn't this supposed to be serious?"
B

Quiet · dashboard-only stats

Zero in-mode surfacing. Streak, achievements, XP all live in the Dashboard page. Drill and Learn show no gamification UI at all.

Pros: Pure. Respects pros.
Cons: Loses the daily-habit loop. Streak that's invisible doesn't motivate the comeback.
C
⭐ MY RECOMMENDATION

Calibrated · visible but adult

3-tier philosophy:

① ALWAYS VISIBLE · subtle pill in shell chrome
🔥 7 · ★ 12/36
Only two numbers: streak days and patterns mastered. No XP number visible. Single pill, right side of top nav. Clickable → opens Dashboard.
② MOMENT-OF-EARNING · celebrate, then fade
Unlocks achievements: toast in bottom-right, 4s, with rarity glow — "🎖️ Achievement: Pattern Sensei (Rare) — 10 patterns mastered". One tap to share or dismiss.

Streak milestones (7, 14, 30, 100): subtle full-screen moment with confetti on milestone crossings only. Not every day.

Drill 90+: (already in Q10) tier-based confetti.

Never: XP bursts on every correct answer. No level-ups. No sound effects by default.
③ DASHBOARD PAGE · deep stats for the curious
Full gamification surface lives at /dashboard: XP total, levels (if kept), achievement grid with locked/unlocked + progress toward locked ones, streak history heatmap, skill radar, FSRS review calendar. Opt-in depth for gamification lovers.
XP verdict: Keep in DB (don't rip out — it's already there) but don't surface it anywhere except Dashboard. Streak and "patterns mastered" are the ambient signals. XP is the power-user detail.
Pros: Streak creates daily habit without looking juvenile. Achievement moments feel earned. Dashboard satisfies gamification-curious without polluting main surfaces. Tone matches interview-prep audience.
Cons: Less "dopamine per second" than Duolingo. That's a feature.
D

User-configurable · toggle in settings

Settings has a "Gamification volume" slider: Off / Subtle / Full. User picks their preference.

Pros: Everyone happy.
Cons: 3x UI states to maintain. Most users never touch settings — default still matters. Punt the real decision.
💡 WHY C · ADULT GAMIFICATION
Interview-prep pros are the target persona. They'll check the platform at 11pm after work. XP bursts annoy. Streak fire motivates. Know the difference.

"Patterns mastered X/36" is the best progress metric. Concrete, meaningful, career-relevant ("I've mastered 22 of 36 GoF patterns"). Shareable ("look what I've learned") — unlike XP which is meaningless to anyone outside.

Streak is the adult habit loop. Not "don't break your streak or your league drops!" but quiet "🔥 7". Users who care, see it. Users who don't, ignore it. Jiff 30-day milestone lands because it's earned.

Dashboard for gamification-depth users. The 5% who love stats get their corner. The 95% who want to learn get a clean workspace.
