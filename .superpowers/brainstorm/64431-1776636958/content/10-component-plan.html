💡 KEY INSIGHT
The math shows why Approach 3 is cheap: 20+ files reused untouched, only 8 surgical modifications, and 28 new files most of which are composition (layouts, hooks) rather than logic. The grading engine, hint system, A* router, Dagre layout, patterns data, problems data, Java codegen, FSRS, WalkthroughPlayer checkpoints — all zero lines of new logic. We're rewiring existing power, not building new power.
