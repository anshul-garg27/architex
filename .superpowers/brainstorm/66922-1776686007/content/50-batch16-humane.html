F12

Graceful sunset · how we remove features without betraying trust

Every product eventually removes a feature. Most apps do it rudely — vanish, migrate, break links. Policy: "When we sunset anything, 30-day notice, data export, migration guide, direct email." No surprise. Trust scales across years only if you earn it consistently.

🌅 FEATURE SUNSET POLICY
30d before: Email + in-app notice
14d before: Export button prominently shown
7d before: Migration guide with alternatives
Day 0: Feature disabled, graceful redirect, support link
+90d after: Data still retrievable on request
