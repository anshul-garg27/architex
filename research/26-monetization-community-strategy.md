# Monetization, Community & Go-to-Market Strategy

> Complete business strategy: pricing, open source model, launch playbook, and growth tactics.

---

## PRICING TIERS

### Free (Forever, No Credit Card)
- All Foundations content
- 5 system design simulations/month
- Basic algorithm visualizations (arrays, trees, hash maps)
- Community access (Discord, GitHub)
- Self-hosted via open source (full functionality)

### Pro — $12/mo (annual $144/yr) or $19/mo
- Unlimited simulations (75+ problems)
- Advanced algorithm visualizations (graphs, DP, segment trees)
- LLD/OOD problems (65+)
- Interview mode with AI feedback
- Spaced repetition system
- Offline access
- **Lifetime: $349**

### Team — $9/user/mo (min 3 users, annual)
- Shared workspaces, team dashboards
- Mock interview pairing
- Custom learning paths
- Team leaderboards
- SSO for 10+

### Enterprise — Custom (~$25/user/mo)
- Custom content modules
- Skills assessment & reporting
- LMS integration (SCORM/LTI)
- SOC 2, SLA, on-premise option

## OPEN SOURCE STRATEGY

### License: AGPL-3.0 (core) + Proprietary (cloud)
- `architex-core/` — AGPL-3.0 (engine, visualization, curriculum)
- `architex-cloud/` — Proprietary (hosting, teams, AI, analytics)
- `architex-content/` — CC BY-SA 4.0 (educational content)

Why AGPL: Prevents AWS/Google from cloning as a service. Same strategy as Supabase, GitLab, Plausible.

### Contributor Ladder
1. Contributor (1+ merged PR)
2. Regular Contributor (5+ PRs, active)
3. Maintainer (review access)
4. Core Team (shapes roadmap)

## LAUNCH STRATEGY

### Pre-Launch (6-8 weeks before)
- Build in public on Twitter (#BuildInPublic)
- 3-5 SEO blog posts with embedded interactive simulations
- 2-3 YouTube videos showing simulation
- Target 1,000+ email waitlist

### Week 1-2: Hacker News "Show HN"
- Title: "Show HN: Architex — Open-source system design simulator"
- Tuesday/Wednesday morning launch
- Live demo ready — HN users want to try immediately
- Be in comments 4-6 hours (60% more traffic when founder active)
- Zero marketing language, technical substance only

### Week 3-4: Product Hunt
- 500+ PH followers pre-launch
- Target: 50 upvotes + 30 comments in first 2 hours
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- Offer: "Pro free 30 days for PH community"

### Month 2-6: Content Flywheel
- Monday: Blog post (SEO)
- Wednesday: YouTube video
- Friday: Twitter/X thread
- Weekly newsletter with problem-of-the-week

## KEY PRINCIPLES

1. **Be the Excalidraw of system design** — open source, generous free tier, premium cloud
2. **Undercut on price, overdeliver on interactivity** — $144/yr vs $189+ for competitors
3. **Content is the moat** — interactive simulations are unshareable by competitors
4. **Community before monetization** — build to 50K users, then monetize aggressively
5. **Product-led growth** — developers must try it in 30 seconds, no sales calls
